1.

MC Donalds

Japan 350-1103 Saitama, Kawagoe, Kasumigasekihigashi, 1 Chome-1-4

MCdonalds is an American corporation that operates one of the largest chains of fast food restaurants in the world. MCdonalds is super popular in Jappan. In Japan you will find MCdonald's outlets in every big and small city. You can find a MCdonalds outlet on the first floor of Kasumigaseki Station where they sell burgers, french fries, Coffees, shakes, Soft drinks sandwich, Muffins ets.

Recommendation: Double Cheeseburger

2.

Matsuya Foods

1 Chome-2-22 Kasumigasekihigashi, Kawagoe, Saitama 350-1103, Japan

Matsuya is one of the most popular and busiest chain shops in Japan. Matsuya Foods Co. is a chain of restaurants, including Matsuya, teishoku, and soups. Matsuya is also available in front of Kasumigaseki station. If you want to experience original Japanese foods and culture you must go to Matsuya.

Recommendation: Gyumeshi

3.

Namaste Nippon

Japan 350-1103 Saitama, Kawagoe, Kasumigasekihigashi, 1 Chome-3-24

Are you a South Asian who is Facing problems with getting used to with Japanese taste? Are you Muslim who is having difficulties finding a Halal restaurants? I personally faced those problems. Well I have good news for you. A halal Indians restaurant named Namaste Nippon is 5 minutes walking distance from the Campus. They offer Nan, Curry, Rice sets, Biriyani, Lacchi etc at a reasonable price. If you are not south asian but want to experience South Asian authentic food I'd highly recommend you to visit this restaurant once.

Recommendation: Biriyani
